About Habit Tracker

Build Better Habits, One Day at a Time

Habit Tracker is a simple web application designed to help you build and maintain positive habits through consistent tracking and visualization.

How it Works
  1. Create Habits - Start by adding the habits you want to track daily
  2. Check-in Daily - Log your completion of each habit every day
  3. Build Streaks - Watch your streak grow as you maintain consistency
  4. Add Notes - Optionally add notes to track your progress or feelings
  5. Stay Consistent - Don't break the chain!
The Science Behind Habit Formation

Research shows it takes anywhere from 18 to 254 days to form a new habit, with an average of 66 days for a behavior to become automatic. Consistency is key - tracking your habits visually helps maintain motivation and creates a sense of achievement.

Tip: The 2-Day Rule

Never miss a habit two days in a row. Missing one day is a mistake; missing two days is the start of a new habit.
